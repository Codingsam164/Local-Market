Details About Our Website Is Given Here

To empower local shop owners since they were getting neglected owing to variable crop patterns and rising prices, we decided to provide them with a platform designed specifically for them. Moreover, after Covid began, people found it difficult to step out of their houses and hence an online platform was necessary so that the local people can get groceries easily from their neighbourhood using just a phone!

To scale the website and the bussinesses of the grocers. Also, to provide a better interface for the shop owners and the people buying from it as well. We are developing everyday and we try to make this platform easier to use and more and more convenient on a day to day basis. We understand the importance of fresh vegetables and it's requirement and hence we try to update our website as often as possible. Having said that, we are open for suggestions and improvisations!

Contact us from the "Contact Us " section of the website. Our team will assist you with everything. We are all here to assist you but just in case we do not reply to your mails, feel free to call any of the helplines given in the contact us web pages.

We will be launching the app soon and we're working on it for a long time. We keep the convenience of our customers and users on the first priority hence we will try to release the app as early as possible. Check here for more updates!

Currently, we just tend to local vendors and dealers serving to the neighbourhood. But with more acknowledgement and responce, we will definitely try to increase the span as and when our resources permit us to do so! We hope to bring as many local vendors on this platform just for their encouragement and to help them in whichever way posssible in these testing times. Considering the abnormal production and the changing prices, there is a lot they have to go through and we need fresh vegetables as well, so this platform aims at to reduce the stress of both the producers and the consumers by reducing the distance between them.
